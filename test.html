The Philosophy of CROW

An age ago, a crow was birthed in the north of England. This was no ordinary crow, as they were soon to discover they had extraordinary powers.

'Pon the Crow's day of fledge, a third and fourth wing began to form from the lateral and medial aspects of it's body. Other crows in the murder cawed with horror, shock and wonder. The legend of the quad-wing crow was a fable, something told to crow chicks to keep the Owl-based nightmares at bay. Little did any know that myth was about to become reality.
